About

Magdalena Ponce de León is an independent neuroimaging consultant and PhD candidate in Clinical Neurosciences with 6+ years of experience in clinical PET/MRI workflows, quantitative imaging, and advanced data analysis. She works with PET centers and clinical teams across South America and Europe to convert imaging data into reliable, actionable results that support patient diagnosis and care.

Her technical expertise includes MATLAB, Python, R, SPM12, FSL, FreeSurfer, ANTs, and PMOD, enabling development of tailored, reproducible pipelines for preprocessing, VOI segmentation, and quantification that improve diagnostic precision.

Multilingual (Spanish, English, Italian, German) and experienced in international, multidisciplinary collaborations, Magdalena provides consultancy to imaging centers aiming to optimize diagnostic workflows and reporting.
