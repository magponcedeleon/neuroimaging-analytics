Veralia: Complete Clinical Tool for PET

Designed for physicians, Veralia improves image quality, quantifies VOIs, aligns PET with anatomical scans, compares patient data to normal databases, tracks multi-visit studies, and is extremely easy to use.

1. Gold-Standard Motion Correction

Eliminate movement artifacts in PET time-series using rigid correction, ensuring reliable SUV metrics.

2. PET-to-Anatomical Image Alignment

Align PET scans with anatomical images such as MRI or CT for precise localization and improved diagnostic accuracy.

3. Accurate, Automated VOI Metrics

Standardized SUVmean and SUVmax calculation across all required volumes of interest, reducing observer variability and saving time.

4. Multi-Visit Patient Tracking

Automatically align and compare VOI values across multiple visits for robust longitudinal disease monitoring.

5. Intuitive, Time-Saving Workflow

Designed for physicians: an easy-to-use interface that accelerates workflow and allows quick, confident decisions without losing time.

Make Confident Clinical Decisions with PET Data

Quickly diagnose patients using motion-corrected, quantified, and longitudinally comparable PET data, compared against validated normal databases—all within an intuitive interface designed for physicians.
