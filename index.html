About Me

I’m a Bioengineer and PhD candidate in Clinical Neurosciences with over 6 years of experience in neuroimaging, digital health, and advanced data analysis.

As a freelance neuroimaging consultant, I help PET centers, clinical teams, and research groups by transforming complex imaging data into clear, actionable insights. Multilingual (Spanish, English, Italian, German) and experienced in working across multidisciplinary, international teams in Argentina and Austria.

My work is built on strong technical skills (MATLAB, SPM12, R, Python, FreeSurfer, ANTs, FSL, PMOD) and a passion for bridging the gap between data and clinical decision-making.
