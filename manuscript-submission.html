📈 Manuscript Submission & Publication

Journal selection guidance, formatting, online submission handling, preprint uploads, and tracking.

Navigating the publication process can be complex and time-consuming. I provide dedicated support to ensure your manuscript meets all requirements and reaches the right audience efficiently.

📚 Journal Selection Guidance

I help identify the most appropriate journals for your research based on scope, impact, open access policies, and acceptance rates — maximizing your chances of acceptance.

📝 Manuscript Formatting & Compliance

Your manuscript will be formatted to meet the target journal’s style guidelines, including references, figures, tables, and supplementary materials — avoiding common pitfalls and delays.

💻 Online Submission Handling

I manage the technical side of submission portals, uploading files correctly, filling required metadata, and ensuring all forms and disclosures are completed accurately.

🔍 Submission Tracking & Communication

Stay informed with regular updates on your manuscript status. I help communicate with editors and reviewers, facilitating responses and revisions as needed.
